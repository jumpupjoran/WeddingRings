Blockchain-Verified Love Stories

Where digital vows, emotional memories, and real value come together — welcome to the future of wedding rings.

Pitch: Imagine a future where a wedding ring isn’t just a symbol, but a verifiable, programmable, and valuable asset.

What Is It?

Our NFT Wedding Ring App lets couples mint unique blockchain-based wedding rings — public proof of commitment, backed optionally with real crypto value, and embedded with personal vows, messages, and more.

Key Features

  • Immutable NFT Wedding Rings – Symbol of marriage on-chain, permanent and verifiable.
  • Attach Vows & Messages – Embed love letters, time-locked vows, and family wishes.
  • On-chain Guest Witnesses – Guests get "witness NFTs" as digital proof of presence.
  • Wedding Fundraising via Apple Pay – Guests can fund the couple using fiat, seamlessly converted to crypto.

Tokenization of Emotion & Value

Rings can be backed by ETH, BTC, or SOL — making them not only symbolic but also financially meaningful. The NFTs can later be used to represent divorce, inheritance, or emergency value extraction.

Market Potential

  • Integration into wedding venues and planners as an upsell
  • White-label licensing to SaaS wedding platforms
  • Viral potential through emotional sharing and digital guestbooks

Why It Matters

This isn’t just another NFT project. It's about modernizing one of the most emotional human traditions, preserving it forever in a transparent, decentralized format.
